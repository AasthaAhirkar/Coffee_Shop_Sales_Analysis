# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive *Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard* designed to analyze retail sales data and generate actionable business insights.
This project helps understand customer behavior, sales trends, product performance, and store-level comparisons using visual analytics.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

The main objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data to gain actionable insights that enhance the overall performance of the coffee shop and support data-driven decision-making.

---
## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Excel / CSV Dataset
- DAX Measures
- Data Cleaning & Modeling

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

*Key KPIs:*
- *Total Sales:* $698,812.33
- *Total Footfall:* 149,116
- *Average Bill per Person:* 4.69
- *Average Orders per Person:* 1.44

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### ⏰ Sales by Hour
- Peak order quantity during *7 AM – 10 AM*
- Strong morning coffee demand

### 📅 Sales by Day
- *Friday* has the highest footfall
- *Saturday* shows lower customer visits

### 🗓 Monthly Analysis
- Month-wise filtering to identify seasonal trends

### 🏬 Store Location Performance
- Locations analyzed:
  - Astoria
  - Hell’s Kitchen
  - Lower Manhattan
- Hell’s Kitchen shows slightly higher sales with similar footfall

---

## 🧁 Category-wise Sales Distribution

- Coffee contributes the highest share of total sales
- Tea and Bakery items are strong secondary contributors
- Packaged items have minimal contribution

---

## ☕ Top Products Based on Sales

1. Barista Espresso
2. Brewed Chai Tea
3. Gourmet Brewed Coffee
4. Hot Chocolate
5. Brewed Black Tea

---

## 🥤 Order Size Distribution

- Regular and Large sizes dominate orders
- Small size has the least contribution

## 💡 Business Insights

- Identified peak hours for better staff planning
- Highlighted top-selling products for promotions
- Store-wise insights support location-based strategy
